131 teams. 12 weeks of action. All fighting to be the best. This is NCAA Football.
In this game, you will sim a season at a time as the head coach (very basic) of any of the 131 teams.
Which team you choose is up to you.
You will be assigned 12 games to fill out your "season".
Note: The postseason simulation is not working right now. It will be implemented in the future.
6 wins gets you a bowl
10 wins gets you a playoff berth (top 4)
Disclaimer: Due to CPU limitations, simultaneous simulations cannot be run, so ranking will not be implemented for now.
